## COMMISSION TRACKING & BOOKING PROCEDURES

### For Staff When Customer Calls:
1. **Listen to Request:** What activity/service are they interested in?
2. **Check List:** Find matching partner in this document
3. **Coordinate:** Contact partner directly using provided information
4. **Confirm Details:** Dates, times, group size, special requests
5. **Quote to Customer:** Provide price (partner price + your commission)
6. **Book:** Confirm with partner and customer
7. **Track Commission:** Log booking date, partner, amount, commission earned
8. **Follow-up:** Ensure customer satisfaction, gather testimonials

### Commission Calculation Example:
- Partner charges: 1,000 baht for kayaking tour
- Your commission: 25% = 250 baht
- Customer pays you: 1,250 baht
- You pay partner: 1,000 baht
- Your profit: 250 baht

### Key Message to Customers:
"We handle all the coordination so you get a seamless experience. Just call us, tell us what you want to do, and we'll arrange everything. Our partners are trusted, vetted, and we personally recommend them."

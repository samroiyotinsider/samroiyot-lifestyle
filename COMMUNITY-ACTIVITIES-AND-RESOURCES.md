# SAM ROI YOT - COMMUNITY ACTIVITIES & RESOURCES
## For Staff Research & Contact Development

**Purpose:** Activities that exist but lack formal booking structures. Staff should use these resources to establish direct contacts and verify current operations.

## COMMUNITY-ORGANIZED ACTIVITIES

### Beach Cleanup & Environmental Service
- **Description:** Organized beach cleanup events with environmental education
- **Frequency:** Monthly (varies)
- **How to Find:** Search Facebook group "Friends of Sam Roi Yot"
- **Resources:**
  - Facebook Group: https://www.facebook.com/groups/friendsofsamroiyot/
  - Search posts for "beach cleanup" or "environmental"
- **Action Items:** Contact group admins to get current organizer contact info
- **Commission:** 10-15% (if applicable)

### Saturday Morning Beach Exercise (#samroiyotrun)
- **Description:** Community fitness activity on the beach
- **When:** Every Saturday at 7:00 AM
- **How to Find:** Local community Facebook groups
- **Resources:**
  - Friends of Sam Roi Yot: https://www.facebook.com/groups/friendsofsamroiyot/
  - Search for "samroiyotrun" or "beach exercise"
- **Action Items:** Contact group members or post inquiry
- **Commission:** 10-15% (if applicable)

### Ban Phu Yai Traditional Broom-Making Workshop
- **Description:** Visit and workshop at traditional broom-making community
- **Location:** Ban Phu Yai, Sam Roi Yot area
- **How to Find:** Local community connections
- **Resources:**
  - Facebook mention: https://www.facebook.com/groups/435992023949535/posts/1422666268615434/
  - Post mentions "small community round the corner in Ban Phu Yai"
- **Action Items:** Contact local guides or tour operators who may know the community
- **Commission:** 15-25%

## MARKET TOURS & FOOD EXPERIENCES

### Longlae Market - Saturday Beach Market
- **Description:** Guided tour of Saturday beach market with local food and live music
- **When:** Every Saturday 5:30 PM - 11:30 PM
- **Location:** Southern end of Beach Road, Sam Roi Yot
- **How to Find:** Community event (no formal operator)
- **Resources:**
  - Visit Sam Roi Yot: https://www.visitsamroiyot.com/events/long_lay_new_year_market/
  - Facebook: https://www.facebook.com/groups/friendsofsamroiyot/
  - Instagram: Search "Longlae Market" or "Long Lay Market"
- **Commission:** 15-20% (negotiate with market organizers)
- **Action Items:** Contact market organizers directly or through community group

### Local Fresh Market Tours
- **Description:** Daily fresh market tours with local guide
- **How to Find:** Through local guides or tour operators
- **Resources:**
  - Visit Sam Roi Yot: https://www.visitsamroiyot.com/events/local-food-market/
  - TripAdvisor: https://www.tripadvisor.com/Attraction_Review-g2543854-d15022930-Reviews-Local_Fresh_Market-Sam_Roi_Yot_Prachuap_Khiri_Khan_Province.html
  - Hua Hin Street Food Tours: https://www.getyourguide.com/hua-hin-l911/hua-hin-35-hour-tasty-thai-food-market-walking-tour-t418989/
- **Commission:** 15-20%
- **Action Items:** Contact local guides or Hua Hin tour operators who may extend to Sam Roi Yot

- **How to Arrange:** Through tour operators
- **Resources:**

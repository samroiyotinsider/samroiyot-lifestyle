**VOICEOVER:**

"After 30 years, the lease ends. You can renew it, but you don't own the land."

[Cut to: Freehold property]

"It's yours. Forever. No expiration date."

**FREEHOLD ADVANTAGES:**
"Complete ownership and control."

"Easier to finance and refinance."

"Simpler to sell or transfer."
